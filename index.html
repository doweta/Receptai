Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  3. Stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda in a large bowl.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  6. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool in pans for 10 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
